For example, this is my second paragraph. You’ll delete it and put your own here, hopefully! And now, here’s a graphic: I make sure to include both a screenshot and the Datawrapper “Share & Embed” link  (you’ll need to be logged in to Datawrapper to get this URL).

Here’s another paragraph of text. Remember, you’re shooting for at least two graphics , so your best basic structure is going to be text, image, text, image, text. Since you have two weeks, it would be a great idea to do a little bit of reporting to show that you do more than just lean on datasets! But sometimes your data is going to be awful and you just won’t have enough time, and that’s okay.

Notice how this second chart has a noticeable difference from the default DataWrapper style.  When you’re doing visualization, you usually want to tweak the style somewhat to make it your own. My recommendation is to start with color . For example, think about how The Economist has such a strong color scheme  which makes its charts noticeably The Economist-y . Can you develop your own style? Good luck!
